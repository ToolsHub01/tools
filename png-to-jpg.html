Why Convert PNG to JPG?

PNG and JPG are two of the most common image formats, each with its own advantages:

PNG (Portable Network Graphics)

  • Supports transparency (alpha channel)
  • Lossless compression - no quality loss when saving
  • Larger file sizes compared to JPG
  • Best for graphics, logos, and images with text

JPG (Joint Photographic Experts Group)

  • Uses lossy compression - smaller file sizes
  • No transparency support
  • Ideal for photographs and complex images
  • Widely supported by all devices and browsers

Our tool lets you convert PNG to JPG while controlling the quality to find the perfect balance between file size and image clarity.
