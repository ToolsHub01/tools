Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our video optimization tool

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our video optimization tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges.

Are my videos uploaded to your servers?

No, all processing happens directly in your browser. Your videos never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy.

What video formats are supported?

We support all major formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM, and more. The output formats available are MP4, WebM, and MOV.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no strict limit, but very large files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.

How much can I reduce my video file size?

Compression results vary, but typically you can reduce file size by 50-80% with minimal quality loss.
